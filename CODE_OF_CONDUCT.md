# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting
Report incidents to project maintainers through repository security or issue channels.
